Solve the puzzle by using your mouse to drag and drop puzzle pieces onto the grid on the left. If you want to use a keyboard, press the spacebar to alternate between SELECT PIECE mode and MOVE PIECE mode. While in MOVE PIECE mode, use your arrow keys to move the selected piece up, down, left, or right. Drop the piece by pressing the spacebar again. You cannot drop one piece on top of another. To select a different piece, go to SELECT PIECE mode and press the left or right arrow keys. Puzzled? Click the Solve It button to show the solution. Want to play again? Click the Jumble It button.
