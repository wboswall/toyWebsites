The Spice Bowl Recommends

Salt-Roasted Pecans

  • 2 cups pecans
  • 3 Tbs. butter, melted
  • 1 1/4 tsp. fine sea salt

Preheat oven to 325°. Toss pecans and butter together; add salt and toss again. Spread in a single layer on a baking sheet. Bake about 15 minutes. Cook on baking sheet.

Dried Beef Sticks

  • 5 lbs. ground chuck
  • 5 Tbs. quick salt
  • 3 tsp. mustard seed
  • 2 tsp. granulated garlic
  • 2 tsp. cracked pepper
  • 1 Tbs. hickory smoked salt
  • 1 Tbs. liquid smoke

Mix beef and season. Place in a large covered container for three days; make sure beef is thoroughly mixed each day. On third day, shape beef into sticks, and bake at 150° for 8 hours (turn half-way through.)
