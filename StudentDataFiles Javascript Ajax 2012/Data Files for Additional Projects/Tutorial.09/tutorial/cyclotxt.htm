The lure of the open road is upon us again as CycloCrossings starts another year of great tours and cycling adventures. Regardless of your age or experience, make this your year to explore the United States on bicycle. We have a 20-year history of providing great tours with expert tour guides. Or if you prefer, set off on your own with one of our tour books.

We've added several new tours this year. If you love to stop and sample wine during your tour, check out the Wine Country Special tour. For a great adventure, join us for the Alaskan Audax tour during June and July. Finally, be sure to explore the Idaho Redux with a new twist on an old favorite.

Are you new to CycloCrossings? Go to our membership page for great savings and access to special members-only features.
