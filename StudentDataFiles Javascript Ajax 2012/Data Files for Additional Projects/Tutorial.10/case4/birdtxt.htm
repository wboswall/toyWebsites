Where Both the Good and Bad Enjoyed a Night Out

Now a museum, the Birdcage was once the "bawdiest, most wicked honky-tonk" in America (New York Times). Miners - and other folks - would drop in to the Birdcage for a drink, a show, gambling, and any of a number of other vices. You should also drop in to see one of the most interesting displays of old west memorabilia you'll find anywhere.
