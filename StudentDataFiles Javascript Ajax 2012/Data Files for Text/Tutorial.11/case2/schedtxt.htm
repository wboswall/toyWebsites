National News

Get the latest in national and regional news from the LSN (Listener-Supported Network) with your hosts Gary Li and Karen Kurthoven.

Local News

Keep up with the latest news from North Dakota with your hosts Steve Greene and Marty Miller.

Opera Fest

This week Opera Fest presents extended excerpts from Turandot by Giacomo Puccini. Join us for the fun.

Radio U

Novelist Karen Graves reads from her latest work, Hellion of Troy and discusses how to get started in the publishing world.

Science Week

Prof. Thomas Glass from UND Dept. of Physics discusses String Theory and Spooky Action at a Distance.

The Living World

Join us for a panel discussion on the Return of the Electric Car and the latest in eco-news.

Word Play

Join Kent Whyte, Donna Verdann, Bill Trudea, and Linda Jacobsen for game about words.

Brain Stew

Expand your mind with the latest news from the world of speculative science and alternate realities presented by host Yale Crawford.

Agri-Week

Keep informed and up-to-date on the latest agricultural news and trends from North Dakota with your host Harold Hawkes.

Bismarck Forum

Join us for a discussion of land use issues and the future of sustainable growth in North Dakota.

Folk Fest

Join host Fiona McKenzie with the latest in traditional and contempory folk music, live from the Bismarck Auditorium.

Classical Music Connection

Bob Lee plays a nightly selection of classical music favorites. A KPAF standard for the past twenty years.

Classical Music Connection

The Shadow, Fibber McGee, Little Orphan Annie and many others live again for your listening pleasure.

Saturday Nite Jazz

Tune in for the best of Jazz: from Swing and Be-Bop to contempory favorites; all served up with fun facts and stories by Jazz historian, Deke Pauls.

The Indie Connection

Keep up with the latest trends and newest stars as we explore the new voices of independent music with your host, Gale Sweeney.

World News Feed

End the day with a global perspective as we report news from the BBC, International Wire Service, and other global news organizations.
